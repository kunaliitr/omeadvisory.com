Our Differentiation

Why the inside view
changes everything.

Most ESG consultants advise from one side of the regulatory fence. OME Advisory brings direct experience as a rule-maker (UNFCCC), a standards verifier (SGS, TÜV network), and a corporate practitioner (MSCI, Uniper, GE).

That three-sided perspective means we understand not just what the regulations say — but how they were written, where they flex, and how they will be enforced.

Regulatory Insider Knowledge

Former UNFCCC P-4 experience means we've read the negotiating text, not just the summary.

ESG Ratings Methodology Fluency

As former MSCI ESG Research Lead, we know exactly how raters assess what companies disclose — and what they don't.

Implementation-First Delivery
